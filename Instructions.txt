操作説明
---------------------------

Space/Enter/Z: 決定・会話・探索
Shift: 押しっぱなしでダッシュ
X/Esc: キャンセル、メニュー


F1: ゲームプロパティ
F2: FPS表示
F12: タイトル画面へ戻る



Button Configurations
---------------------------

Space/Enter/Z: Confirm, Talk, Examine
Shift: Hold down to dash
X/Esc: Cancel, Menu


F1: Game Properties
	The menu is in Japanese, so I'll translate here.
The 1st tab has 4 options:
	start-up on fullscreen
	some graphics option(I don't see any difference)
	BGM on/off
	SE on/off
I suggest you don't change the gamepad (tab 2) or keyboard (tab 3)
options.

F2: Display FPS
F12: Return to Title
